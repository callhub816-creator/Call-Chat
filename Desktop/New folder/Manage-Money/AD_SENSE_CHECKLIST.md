## Overview

This checklist ensures Manage Money Online meets Google AdSense approval requirements before submitting your application. AdSense has strict policies around content quality, originality, traffic source, and compliance. This checklist covers all known requirements as of December 2025.

**Important:** Google's policies change. Before applying, visit [AdSense Help Center](https://support.google.com/adsense) to confirm current requirements.

## Pre-Application Requirements

### Website Basics ✓

- [ ] **Domain registered:** Site accessible at custom domain (not subdomain or free platform)
- [ ] **HTTPS enabled:** Full SSL certificate (all pages secure)
- [ ] **Mobile responsive:** Site renders correctly on mobile devices
- [ ] **Fast loading:** <3 seconds typical load time (test with PageSpeed Insights)
- [ ] **No major errors:** Google Search Console shows no critical indexing errors
- [ ] **Sitemap.xml:** Published and submitted to Search Console
- [ ] **Robots.txt:** Properly configured to allow Googlebot
- [ ] **Analytics enabled:** Google Analytics 4 or alternative tracking (shows legitimate traffic)

### Content Requirements ✓

#### Quantity & Quality
- [ ] **Minimum 15+ high-quality articles** (25+ ideal for approval)
- [ ] **Average article length: 900+ words** (depth = quality signal)
- [ ] **Unique content:** 100% original (no scraped, plagiarized, or spun content)
- [ ] **Well-written:** Professional tone, no obvious grammar/spelling errors
- [ ] **Evergreen focus:** Content remains relevant long-term (not just news)
- [ ] **Regular updates:** Show recent publish/update dates (not stale content)

#### Topic Focus & Niche
- [ ] **Finance/Personal Finance niche:** Clear, consistent topical focus
- [ ] **No prohibited niches:** Avoid gambling, adult content, illegal services
- [ ] **Original take:** Content distinguishes itself from competitors (not generic rewrite)
- [ ] **Expertise evident:** Articles show knowledge, not surface-level information

#### Accurate Information
- [ ] **Facts verified:** All numeric facts, statistics, rates from reliable sources
- [ ] **Sources cited:** Articles link to authoritative sources
- [ ] **[CRITICAL]** **No medical/health claims:** Finance content only (no "this will cure X")
- [ ] **Disclaimers included:** Finance articles include "not financial advice" disclaimers
- [ ] **Regularly updated:** Site shows active maintenance and accuracy reviews

#### User Experience
- [ ] **Clear navigation:** Easy to find content, no confusing menu structure
- [ ] **Fast navigation:** Internal links organized logically (related content visible)
- [ ] **No auto-pop-ups:** Popups not blocking content on first visit
- [ ] **Readable fonts:** Large enough (14px minimum for body text), good contrast
- [ ] **Proper images:** Images load, have alt text, add value (not decorative clutter)
- [ ] **No excessive ads:** Not stuffed with ads; respects user experience
- [ ] **Mobile first:** Mobile version fully functional (not stripped-down)

- [ ] **No guarantees:** Never guarantee returns, savings rates, or investment performance
- [ ] **Neutral language:** No country-specific financial advice (complies with Google policy)

## Common Denial Reasons & Solutions

### ❌ Insufficient Content
- **Reason:** Site has <10 articles or articles are very short (<300 words)
- **Solution:** Publish 15–25+ articles, 900+ words each, before reapplying

### ❌ Low Traffic / No Analytics
- **Reason:** Google can't verify legitimate traffic
- **Solution:** Set up Google Analytics, build organic traffic for 30+ days, then reapply (aim for 1,000+ monthly visitors)

### ❌ Plagiarism / Copied Content
- **Reason:** Content detected as copy of other sites
- **Solution:** Audit all content with Copyscape; rewrite any flagged sections; submit original content only

### ❌ Thin / Low-Quality Content
- **Reason:** Content appears rushed, surface-level, or AI-generated without review
- **Solution:** Deepen articles with unique insights, original research, examples; improve writing quality

### ❌ Unclear Purpose / Niche
- **Reason:** Site mixes too many unrelated topics (finance + sports + gaming = confusing)
- **Solution:** Focus on finance/personal finance exclusively; remove off-topic content

### ❌ Policy Violations
- **Reason:** Adult content, hate speech, illegal services detected
- **Solution:** Audit and remove all policy-violating content before reapplication

### ❌ Privacy Policy Issues
- **Reason:** Privacy policy missing, incomplete, or inaccurate
- **Solution:** Create comprehensive privacy policy; link from footer on all pages

### ❌ No Contact Information
- **Reason:** No way to contact site owner
- **Solution:** Add contact page with email and/or contact form; link in footer

### ❌ Mobile Usability Issues
- **Reason:** Site not mobile-responsive or mobile experience poor
- **Solution:** Implement mobile-first responsive design; test on real devices

### ❌ Traffic from Non-Organic Sources
- **Reason:** Traffic from bots, click networks, or artificial sources
- **Solution:** Stop paid traffic sources; focus on organic, natural audience growth

## Timeline & Expectations

### Pre-Application Phase (Weeks 1–2)
- Complete all checklist items
- Publish 15–25 high-quality articles
- Set up Google Analytics, verify traffic
- Verify privacy policy and legal pages

### Application Phase (Week 3)
- Submit AdSense application
- Google reviews (24–48 hours typically)
- Approval or denial notification via email

### Potential Denial & Reapplication (Week 4+)
- If denied, identify reason (email explains)
- Fix issues (1–2 weeks typically)
- Wait 2 weeks, reapply
- Repeat until approval (most sites approved within 3 attempts)

### Post-Approval (Week 5+)
- Generate AdSense code and ad unit IDs
- Implement ad code on site
- Monitor CPM and earnings
- Continue publishing quality content

## Post-Approval: Next Steps

Once approved:

1. **Generate ad code** in AdSense dashboard
2. **Create ad units** (recommended sizes: 728x90, 300x250, 336x280)
3. **Implement code** on site (Layout component and article pages)
4. **Test ads display** (use AdSense preview mode, not real clicks)
5. **Monitor earnings** for first month (typically 0–2 weeks to see impressions)
6. **Optimize placement** based on CTR and CPM data
7. **Continue publishing** high-quality content regularly
8. **Follow AdSense policies** to avoid account suspension
